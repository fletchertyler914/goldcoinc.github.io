Gemspace - A open world game engine with crypto currency enabled content monetization network

It is fronted by a vast open world social game powered by user contributed content. In many ways this would be similar to Second Life, except that there would be an actual game, not just random chats in virtual space. Whenever someone plays the game, the engine would be mining in the background and the modder would earn a little cash for their efforts based on how many people are hanging out in their realm. They could even make some extra funds by selling users "in game extras" like bigger badder guns, new magic spells etc.
