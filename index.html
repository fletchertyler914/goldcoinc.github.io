Our Developments

Developing blockchain 3.0 innovations with engineers around the world with over 42 years combined blockchain development experienc. The world is short of blockchain engineers? Not with us!
We create powerful, scalable and secure tools for global solutions. These are some of our kids:

Simplicity Kiosk and Bitcoin42

The first multi-cryptocurrency ATM and payment terminal in the world - Bitcoin42 does blockchain education, events, customer services and is a local exchanger.

Viva - Global medical records system

A global medical records system backed by blockchain technology, with a built in international payment network. Performs deep learning queries across the entire dataset. Trains next generation AI systems to spot signs of chronic disease long before they have a chance to become life threatening issues.

Mkoba

A cross platform browser mining and wallet plugin. All user data is stored client-side and never sent to any servers. Additional with an secure instant messaging system.

Votabit - Voting Recordation System

A method of electronically recording, submitting, securing, tabulating and verifying voting results in real time, and providing 3rd-party oversight and documentation of the same.

Gemspace - Open world game engine with cryptocoin monetization network

Fronted by a vast open world social game powered by user contributed content. Similar to Second Life, except it's an actual game. The engine mines in the background and the modder earns cash based on how many people are hanging out in their realm.

NvesTTech - Wall Street plugged in for the rest of us!

Add a our HTML tag to your page and visitors of the site with the NvesTTech plugin detect it; then it gives the visitor the chance to see and review the offering and contribute. Further it allows for offer discovery.
